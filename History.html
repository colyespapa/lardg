Lard Corporation's history

Lard Corporation was created in 2018 by request from the current Lardish King, Mr Lard Lapudding 1st. He became the CEO, but in 2019, during the midterm elections, he was accused by his political opponents of treason and eventually resigned and his Welsh brother, Mr Lard Lapudding 2nd, became the second CEO. In 2021, Mr Lard Lapudding 1st returned to the company, and he and his brother reached an agreement. This agreement said that Mr Lard Lapudding 2nd resigned from his CEO position and would return it to the founder, Mr Lard Lapudding 1st.

In 2021, after Mr Lard Lapudding 1st became CEO again, Lard Corporation was bought by the Lardish Government, and since then, we have improved the quality of our services.

The creation of the Lardish Republic

In 2020, Mr Lard Lapudding 1st and 2nd wanted to create a country where poverty doesn't exist, where people have the freedom of taking their own choices, and where the people choose their leader, that was what they wanted, and in 2021, the Lardish Republic was founded under the name of the United States of the First Lardish Republic with its capital city being the Central Lardish District.

Lard Corporation and all the services provided by it are owned by the Lardish Government
